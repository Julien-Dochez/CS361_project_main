Here is some helpful information the password recovery page
If you have forgotten your password, enter the email in the first text box provided. Once you press "Send Reset Code", an email will be sent to your account with a reset code. You will also be redirected to the password recovery page after clicking the button.
If you have forgotten your username, enter the email associated with your account and your username will be sent to you through your email.
If you have forgotten your username, password, and email, you will not be able to recover your account.
